Eric Charles

Eric Charles began playing guitar in his teens with Robert Lee Herbert, an alumni of Luther Allison's Blues Revue. Charles performed in Chicago for decades and in Europe for almost a decade. He based himself in Brussels, recording and playing with a selection of Europe’s finest musicians while fronting the Eric Charles Group. Eric toured most recently with The Miracles, featuring Sidney Justin. Eric Offers a range of live music options.

Soul Essential celebrates all the music that changed our lives - that remind us of that first summer we heard those songs together. From 70s New York funk to a potpourri of Chicago Blues and R&B, Soul Essential takes you down memory lane with a blend of select songs, sweet harmony, outstanding groove and professional presentation.
